# HRTechify Team Simulator

## Overview

HRTechify Team Simulator is an AI-inspired, narrative-driven team effectiveness diagnostic platform built around Patrick Lencioni's Five Dysfunctions of a Team framework.

Instead of traditional surveys, participants experience realistic workplace simulations tailored to their Job Function and Job Family. Their decisions influence the story progression, creating an immersive assessment experience that feels like solving a real business challenge.

## Framework

This simulator is inspired by Patrick Lencioni's Five Dysfunctions of a Team:

1. Absence of Trust
2. Fear of Conflict
3. Lack of Commitment
4. Avoidance of Accountability
5. Inattention to Results
